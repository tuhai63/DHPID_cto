Cells, tissues and organs (CTO) inspections

Human cells, tissues and organs (CTO) must meet high safety standards before they can be transplanted into recipients in Canada. The federal government regularly inspects establishments that process or handle CTOs.

To learn more about the CTO inspections we conduct, visit about cell, tissue and organ inspections or browse these inspection results:
